# Workflow 07 — DOE Hypothesis Generation from Patent Evidence

## Purpose

Convert extracted patent intelligence into experiment-ready hypotheses without copying competitor examples.

## Procedure

1. Select high-priority Xs with strong evidence or strategic uncertainty.
2. Select Ys linked to CTQs and available test methods.
3. Identify tradeoffs and interactions.
4. Define ranges based on patents, internal feasibility, and platform constraints.
5. Avoid direct claim-copying. Frame as learning experiments.
6. State evidence basis and limitations.
7. Define decision rules.
