# Y Output Quality Rubric

## High Quality Y
- Clearly measured.
- Unit disclosed.
- Test method disclosed.
- Relevant to CTQ.
- Comparable to internal or standard method.

## Medium Quality Y
- Measured but method incomplete.
- Unit available but conditions unclear.
- Directionally useful but not directly comparable.

## Low Quality Y
- Qualitative claim only.
- No method or unit.
- Marketing-like wording.

## Not Comparable
- Method differs too much from internal method or other patents.
- Conditions not disclosed.
