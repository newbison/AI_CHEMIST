# X Criticality Rubric

## High Criticality
- X is varied in examples or comparative examples.
- X appears in independent claims.
- X is linked to key CTQ-related Ys.
- X is controllable in lab or manufacturing.

## Medium Criticality
- X appears in preferred embodiments or repeated examples.
- X has plausible impact on Y but limited direct data.

## Low Criticality
- X appears only in broad optional lists.
- No direct connection to Y.
- Difficult to control or irrelevant to current platform.

## Unknown
- Insufficient evidence.
