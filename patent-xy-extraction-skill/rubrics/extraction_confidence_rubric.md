# Extraction Confidence Rubric

## High Confidence
- Source text is explicit.
- Unit and method are clear.
- X/Y appears in examples or tables.
- Context and range are clear.
- Minimal ambiguity.

## Medium Confidence
- Source supports extraction but has missing range, unit, method, or context.
- Relationship is plausible but partly confounded.
- Term normalization required judgment.

## Low Confidence
- Based mostly on claims, broad description, or generic list.
- No example support.
- Missing method or range.
- Significant ambiguity.

## Inconclusive
- Evidence conflicts or is insufficient.
- Cannot reliably extract without human review.
