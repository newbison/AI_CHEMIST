# Workflow 01 — Project and Portfolio Intake

## Purpose

Define the project context before technical analysis begins. This prevents the workflow from becoming a generic patent-summary exercise.

## Required Inputs

Ask for or infer the following:

- Project name
- Product category
- Business unit / portfolio
- Platform or material system
- Target region or market
- Customer segment
- Original VOC
- Known competitor products
- Known competitor companies
- Internal platform constraints
- Regulatory or claim constraints
- Manufacturing site or process constraints
- Timeline and decision milestone

## Portfolio Context Classification

Classify the project into one of these replication scopes:

```text
Scope A — Same product family, same platform
Scope B — Same product family, different platform
Scope C — Different product family, same material platform
Scope D — Different product family, different material platform
Scope E — New portfolio / exploratory white-space
```

## Quality Check

Before moving forward, verify:

- The original VOC is preserved.
- The portfolio and platform are identified.
- The target market and customer segment are not assumed silently.
- Missing information is visible.
