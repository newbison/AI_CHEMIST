# Workflow 06 — Feasibility and Risk Screen

## Purpose

Screen proposed technical directions before recommending experiments.

## Required Screens

1. IP risk signal screen
2. Regulatory / claim feasibility screen
3. Manufacturing feasibility screen
4. Supplier / raw material feasibility screen
5. Quality and risk management screen
6. Test-method readiness screen
7. Portfolio transferability screen

## Required Output

```markdown
# Feasibility and Risk Screen

## IP Risk Signals
| Concept / Patent | Potential Overlap | Risk Signal | Legal Review Needed | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|

## Regulatory / Claim Feasibility
| Intended Claim | Evidence Needed | Risk Level | Review Needed |
|---|---|---|---|

## Manufacturing Feasibility
| Proposed Design | Current Capability | Gap | Risk | Action |
|---|---|---|---|---|

## Supplier Feasibility
| Material / Component | Availability | Medical / Product Grade | Supply Risk | Qualification Burden |
|---|---|---|---|---|

## Design Risk Mapping
| Design Change | Failure Mode | Potential Harm / Business Impact | Risk Control | Verification Test |
|---|---|---|---|---|

## Test Method Readiness
| Y Metric | Method Available? | Method Gap | Comparability Concern | Action |
|---|---|---|---|---|

## Portfolio Transferability
| Learning / Concept | Source Platform | Target Platform | Transferability | Required Validation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
```

## Quality Check

If any high-risk item is identified, do not present the experiment as ready for execution. Mark it as requiring review.
