# Learning Confidence Scoring Rubric

## Confidence Formula

Use qualitative judgment based on:

```text
data completeness
statistical strength
replicate quality
test method reliability
material-system relevance
range coverage
consistency with prior evidence
human review status
```

## Labels

### High
- Statistically and practically meaningful result
- Good replication
- Relevant test method
- Relevant material system
- Clear tested range
- Reviewed by scientist

### Medium
- Directionally supported
- Some limitations in sample size, range, or method
- Useful for next DOE, not final design rule

### Low
- Weak evidence
- Exploratory observation
- Limited or questionable method
- Not enough replication

### Inconclusive
- Data does not support a reliable conclusion
- Method deviation or missing data prevents interpretation

## Range Awareness

Never state a learning without its valid range and context.
