# Evidence Quality Scoring Rubric

## Evidence Quality Levels

### High
- Direct experimental data
- Clear test method
- Relevant material system
- Relevant product context
- Units and conditions disclosed
- Source is traceable

### Medium
- Relevant but with method differences
- Partial experimental detail
- Similar but not identical material system
- Directionally useful but not directly comparable

### Low
- Limited data
- Inference-heavy
- Missing method details
- Different product or material context

### Unknown
- Insufficient information to judge

## Required Fields
Every evidence item should include:

```text
source
evidence type
method
material system
range / condition
confidence
limitation
```
